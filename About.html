Who We Are

The Artistic Unified Exchange (AUX) was created to Revolutionize the way independent content (Music, TV, & FILM) is distributed and the costs associated with getting your CONTENT to the masses. AUX has emerged as one of the music industry’s premier Resources that offers services such as Business Development, Distribution, Publishing and other Label Services for new acts, labels, and production companies entering into the entertainment industry. The team at AUX makes sure that its clients get seen and heard by top decision makers in the entertainment industry. Our staff consists of entertainment industry professionals that have contributed to the success of some of the most popular names in the entertainment industry today. With over 75 years of combined experience, the team at AUX, knows how to make Independents MAJOR.

Our Mission

It is our mission is to encourage up and coming unsigned talent to pursue his/her aspirations of becoming a successful entertainer through the means of promotion, exposure, networking, and education. We also provide an opportunity for independent artist(s) and labels to expand their fan base(s) and gain exposure, via our networking, promotion, and performance opportunities.
